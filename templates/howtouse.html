Encryption

On the Encrypt tab in the navigation bar you can encrypt any text in unicode that does not exceed a certain character limit to send to someone.

The character limit depends on the attached image, and right after the attachment, the limit is shown above the text box of the message. The calculation is made in relation to how many pixels the image has, considering the steganography method used and explained in the about tab.

Decryption

On the Decrypt tab in the navigation bar you can decrypt a message someone sent you, and you can also choose the number of characters you want to be read.
